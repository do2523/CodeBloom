Our Mission

Welcome to Code Bloom! We are two passionate UCF students on a mission to help you learn how to code. Whether you're just starting out or looking to expand your skills, we’re here to guide you through the exciting world of programming.

Our names are Daniel Ocampo and Luciano Paredes, and we share a deep love for coding in languages like Python, Rust, C++, TypeScript, and JavaScript. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background, can learn to code and build amazing things. That’s why we founded Code Bloom: to create an accessible, affordable way for anyone to grow their coding abilities.

With our personalized, one-hour tutoring sessions, we’ll work with you to make complex concepts easy to understand. Our goal is to help you build the confidence and skills you need to succeed in today’s tech-driven world.

Join us on this journey, and let’s make coding fun and accessible for all!
